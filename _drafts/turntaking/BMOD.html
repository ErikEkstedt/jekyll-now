Backchannels/Acknowledgements

In the switchboard dataset there are several variants of backchannels. However, these words can be used as more things than backchannels. the word "right" could be an acknowledgment but could also indicate a direction. "Yeah" could be a backchannel but also a filler/answer at the beginning of a user turn. So after we have our candidate words we make sure that they are uttered in isolation. Only a single "yeah" or "right" would be a backchannel but when used inside a turn we assume that it is not a backchannel. Candidate bachchannels
  • "yeah"
  • "right"
  • "oh"
  • "huh"
  • "okay"
  • "mhm"
  • "uh-huh"
  • "um-hum"
  • "hum"
  • "hm"
  • "[laughter-BC]"
